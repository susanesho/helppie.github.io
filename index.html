Why Helppie?

Helppie is a powerful web based application developed by Canadians that helps driving schools to register students for training, create and manage training records, generate invoices and electronically sign student consent forms for booking a road test in the most efficient yet simple and easy manner right on your device. Helppie can help you move from paper based to digital document management and provides you with a full digital trail for compliance audit.

How can Helppie help you?

Student Registration

Online registration improves efficiencies and eliminates unnecessary paperwork and gives you the benefit of having your client’s data kept safely in the client's file. Student registration process also allows driving schools to upload a copy of statement of services and policy statement for student review and acknowledgement during the registration process.

Training Records

Streamline your business operations, save the time, cost and hassle associated with collecting and storing paper based documents. With a simple to use interface and access from any device, you can create and manage training records for your registered students in a few seconds. Each record created is automatically saved on secured cloud based storage and you can have access to training records at your fingertips.

Invoicing

Having a paperless method for invoicing eliminates the need for filing and storage. Generate invoice for each transaction with a single click ensuring accuracy and faster processing. Auto invoice generator not only saves on time, but also saves on the receipt printing costs. Every invoice is automatically connected to the student record and includes the client's initials. Access all the invoices in student training records and save a copy on your device in pdf format.

E-Signature

Speed up your document turnaround time with reliable esignature feature. Esignature is secure, encrypted, and legally binding. Enhance your client experience with the convenience of signing a document right from a handheld device. Documents like training record, student consent form, and policy acknowledgement can be signed in few seconds with a click of button. Electronic signatures save clients’ time and make the whole signing process much more desirable for them
